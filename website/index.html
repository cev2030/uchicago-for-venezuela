Who receives your gift

1001 Ideas, INC

For more than 10 years, 1001 Ideas, INC has worked across Venezuela and Latin America supporting young people through social innovation, development, and institution-building.

They will be responsible for purchasing the relief supplies and delivering support directly to student organizations in Caracas. Every step is meant to be accountable and transparent, so donors can always see where their contributions go.

  • U.S.-registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit
  • 10+ years on the ground in Venezuela & LatAm
  • Direct delivery to student organizations in Caracas
  • Public, itemized record of supplies bought & delivered

Learn more about 1001 Ideas: Instagram · YouTube · Facebook

Tax-deductible giving

If you are a U.S. taxpayer and itemize deductions, your gift is deductible to the extent allowed by law. You receive no goods or services in return, so the full amount generally qualifies.

For any single gift of $250+, request a written acknowledgment by emailing 1001ideasvenezuela@gmail.com.

Verify status via the IRS Tax Exempt Organization Search.

This is general information, not legal or tax advice.
